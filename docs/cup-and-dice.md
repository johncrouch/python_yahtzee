# Cup and dice

## Cup

A collection of dice objects that are waiting for shake and roll

### Shake and roll

The dice in the cup have their values randomly changed

## Keepers

A collection of dice objects that are removed from the cup, and not subject to 'shake and roll'.

## Dice

An object that has 1 of 6 value

- 1
- 2
- 3
- 4
- 5
- 6

# Round

During a round, each [player](player.md) has a turn.

The round is complete when each player has completed their turn.

# Turn

During a turn, a player can [shake and roll](cup-and-dice.md) the [dice](cup-and-dice.md) up to 3 times.


- First [shake and roll](cup-and-dice.md):
    - Place all 5 [dice](cup-and-dice.md) in the [cup](cup-and-dice.md),
    - [Shake and roll](cup-and-dice.md) them out.
    - Set aside any dice you want to keep ([keepers](cup-and-dice.md)).
- Second [shake and roll](cup-and-dice.md):
    - [Shake and Roll](cup-and-dice.md) in the dice in the [cup](cup-and-dice.md).
    - Set aside any dice you want to add to the [keepers](cup-and-dice.md).
- Third [shake and roll](cup-and-dice.md):
    - [Shake and Roll](cup-and-dice.md) the remaining dice in the [cup](cup-and-dice.md) one last time.
    - After this roll, you must record either a score or a zero (0) in one of the 13 spaces on your form. Once the route is filled, your turn is over.

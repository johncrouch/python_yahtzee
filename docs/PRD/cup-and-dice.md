# Cup and dice

## Cup

A collection of [dice](cup-and-dice.md#dice) objects that are waiting for [shake and roll](cup-and-dice.md#shake-and-roll)

### Shake and roll

The [dice](cup-and-dice.md#dice) objects in the cup have their values randomly changed

## Keepers

A collection of [dice](cup-and-dice.md#dice) objects that are removed from the [cup](cup-and-dice.md#cup), and not subject to [shake and roll](cup-and-dice.md#shake-and-roll).

## Dice

An object that has 1 of 6 value

- 1
- 2
- 3
- 4
- 5
- 6
